# TODO - Submission for DevPost Description

Issue: Aspirational Recyclists and other environmentally conscious people (essentially the general public, and growing) want to properly recycle and put items in the right bins. The issue is that currently, people either throw stuff in the trash by default because they dont want to think about where to put it, or people put the wrong stuff in the recycling. With recycling especially, putting the wrong things causes pounds of recycling to have to be thrown away due to contamination. And additionally, each city has different rules for their recycling.

Our product:
* A screen/camera combo that is to be placed on top of major recycle/trash/compost bins, such as in a school dining hall or in major public facilities and areas.
* The camera is constantly scanning for items to be put in front of it, and if it sees an object that is still for some period of time, it snaps a picture, then analyzes what product that is.
    * Upon camera install, it automatically sends its location to the backend server, and then fetches the MRF file for that specific city/location. This will periodically update
* After analyzing and classifying what the item is, a popup shows up on the screen telling the user whether that item is recycling, trash, or compost based on the classification.
* User throws the item away and camera is ready for the next item.

Pain Points
* Aspirational Recyclists want to recycle, but they dont want to spend minutes opening an app on their phone and scanning an object just to throw it away.
    * Our system will be FAST, as in <3 seconds per query.
    * We cannot easily change customer behavior, but we can improve it if we make it seamless and easy to use our product
* In recycling centers currently, depending on the type of contaminant, the center throws away entire portions of material, wasting countless tons of recycling every year. Current scanning systems do not work as well as proper seperation at the people side.
    * Our product will guide people on which bin to throw away trash in
* Current products that are similar are localized to one city, and even these are few and far in between, and are costly to implement
    * Our product will automatically retrieve recyling trash and other guidelines for each city based on the location of the device, making it a general solution that cities can just put out and let work.
